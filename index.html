What is Vinyasa yoga?

Vinyasa yoga is characterized by linking a series of postures together.
Think of it as a moving meditation, it is all about harmony and balance, grace and fluidity. We move and notice how we are moving and what moves us.

Benefits of Vinyasa Yoga

What is Hatha yoga?

Hatha yoga is a branch of yoga. What makes this different from vinyasa yoga?
The pacing of the sequences. Because the pace of this style of practice is slower, and poses are held for longer, it allows for more stretching in each shape, challenging your strength and flexibility while at the same time focusing on relaxation and mindfulness.

What are the benfits of Hatha yoga?

  • Stress reduction
  • Muscle and joint flexibility
  • Core strength
